requently Asked Questions

Everything You Need to Know

Find answers to common EV charging concerns quickly and easily

01 I just got my electric vehicle and I need to know where I can charge it. Can the app show me all available stations nearby in real time?
+
Yes, the app provides real-time updates on all available charging stations near your location.
02 I want to ensure that a charging station will be available when I arrive. Is it possible to reserve a slot in advance to avoid waiting?
+
Yes, reservations can be made in advance depending on station availability.
03 Can I browse stations without registering? Which features require creating an account to access them?
+
Browsing is available without registration, but booking and tracking require an account.
04 I want to avoid running out of battery unexpectedly while driving. Can the app notify me when my battery is low or when a charging session starts or ends?
+
Yes, the app can send notifications for battery status and charging sessions.
05 Do I need to pay to use the basic app features or to search for stations? Are there any additional costs or hidden charges I should be aware of?
+
Basic features are free. Charges only apply when using paid charging services.
06 Once I plug in my EV, I want to monitor how fast it is charging. Does the app provide live updates on battery percentage and estimated time remaining?
+
Yes, live updates show battery percentage and estimated charging time.
07 I own more than one electric vehicle and want to track charging for each car. Can the app handle multiple vehicles and show separate charging details for each?
+
Yes, multiple vehicles can be added and managed separately.
08 What should I do if a charging station is not working or if I encounter a problem with the app? Can I reach customer support quickly and easily?
+
You can report issues directly within the app or contact support anytime.
